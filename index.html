Privacy Policy for Flip-Decide

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Flip-Decide does not collect any personal information. The app is designed to work completely offline and does not require or collect any personal data from users.

2. Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Coin flip history
  • Decision lists and options

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to any external servers. All data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. App Permissions

Flip & Decide uses the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback during coin flips to enhance user experience
  • INTERNET: Only used to display this privacy policy page within the app

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect user information. This includes:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking services
  • No social media integrations

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with children's privacy protection regulations.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • No data is stored on external servers

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can clear your history within the app at any time
  • You can view your coin flip history in the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
