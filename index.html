Privacy Policy for Flip & Decide

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Thank you for using Flip & Decide. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information and permissions.

1. Information We Collect

Flip & Decide does not collect any personal information.

The app works primarily offline and does not require user registration, login, or account creation.

2. Local Device Storage

The app stores certain information locally on your device to improve user experience, including:

  • Coin flip history
  • Decision maker options
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Notification settings

This data is stored only on your device using local storage technologies such as SharedPreferences. No data is uploaded to external servers.

3. Notifications

The app may send optional local notifications/reminders if enabled by the user.

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted
  • You can disable notifications anytime in Settings

4. App Permissions

Flip & Decide may request the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE — Used for haptic feedback during coin flips and interactions.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Used to display reminder notifications (Android 13+).
  • INTERNET — Used only to open external links such as the Privacy Policy page.

5. Third-Party Services

Flip & Decide currently does not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

Specifically, the app does NOT use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • User tracking systems
  • Analytics platforms
  • Social media integrations

6. Children's Privacy

Flip & Decide is safe for users of all ages.

Since the app does not collect personal information, it complies with child privacy protection requirements.

7. Data Security

Since all app data stays on your device:

  • Your data remains under your control
  • No cloud storage is used
  • No external database is connected
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your app data.

  • You can clear history inside the app
  • You can disable notifications anytime
  • You can uninstall the app to remove all data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
